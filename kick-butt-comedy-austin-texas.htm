Comedy Open Mic Night

Wednesday Comedy Open Mic


Every Wednesday from 8-10pm unless there is a holiday or another event.
Check the calendar to make sure if you are uncertain.

The list for the open mic opens at 7:30pm. Justin Biske is our host and he will set a list out on a table. Sign up is first come first served after he puts the list out.

Depending on how many comedians sign up the set times are normally 3-5 minutes. On busier nights you get less time to perform and vice versa. This open mic is for comedy - unless it's a comedy song or poem. For that you should go to the Thursday Music Open Mic or Sunday Poetry Open Mic.

We DO NOT DO CALL IN SIGN UPS!!!!!!! or advance sign ups. You have to be here in person to sign up, even if you know the host or whatever. If you do know the host, you can call or text them directly to get on the list. Kick Butt has no involvement in that.

Verified Comedians get $2 Lone Stars and Shiners.
