"With a name like Kick Butt, you'd think the atmosphere in this coffeehouse would lean toward assertive--or at least jittery. "Kick back" is far more descriptive. Unpretentious and un-corporate, Kick Butt is the creation of Austin's renowned Tai Chi and Kung Fu master, Thomas Gohring (pictured). Here you're just as likely to bump into artists, musicians and poets as you are entrepreneurs, office workers and bean counters.

The art of latte-making has been mastered. Deliciously original "sammiches" named for legends like Jackie Chan are featured on the menu. And don't resist treating yourself to the "Ying Yang" white and black chocolate cake.

Kick Butt has earned its well-deserved black belt in mouth-watering fare and entertaining yet relaxed ambience. The only thing you'll have to fight here is the impulse to sip, eat and linger all day and night." 
- Magic 95.5 FM

HEB Central Market Fresh Toasted Bagels
(with Cream Cheese or Butter)

Plain, 8-Grain, Poppyseed, Cinnamon Raisin Swirl, Jalapeno and Everything Bagel
(Sold each day until we run out)

The bagels are where many coffee shops fall short. Places where bagels are not like bagels at all...more like dinner rolls, soft yeasty things, with no pull or crust. They squish and stay squished, like the soft dinner roll you had at your last thanksgiving dinner.

When you bite into a bagel, you are expecting Chuck Norris, a crust that is tough but yielding, a tough veneer holding a firm interior that is ready to support the soft feminine touch of philly cream cheese or softened butter. A bakery product that might bend at the bite but never ever cave in.

The "dinner roll" bagel is more like the guy who sleeps on your couch and won't leave. It has no crust, a flabby body, and little to no chew, It sticks to the roof of your mouth and has airholes in it like Wonder® Bread ... oy vey!

Our Bagels are prepared the old-world way, boiled and then baked from HEB Central Market. Get it toasted and you'll taste something near perfection: a bagel that is crunchy, not too dense or sweet, and just chewy enough. They are great with cream cheese as breakfast or as an afternoon snack.

Kick’n Chicken Salad Sammich (Our most popular classic sammich)
Spicy Southwest Chicken Salad on the bread of your choice. Our most popular. Master Gohring’s secret recipe. It’s been passed down from Master to Student for 36 generations just so you could enjoy it. Warning! It’s called Kick’n Chicken for a reason (it's really hot) so you may want to add Cheddar or Provolone or a Mexican Coke. Just say'n.

Bruce Lee “Cha-Cha” Chicken Sammich (Our Most popular new sammich)
The “Cha Cha” was Bruce Lee’s favorite dance. We think this will become your favorite sammich. Hot Diced Chicken with Melted Cheddar and our Delicious Creamy Jalapeño Cilantro Sauce on Ciabatta

Return of the Dragon Sammich (pictured left)
Why Return of the Dragon Sammich? Because fans of the film will never forget when Bruce Lee defeated...Chuck Norris! Bruce was the only one that could do it. This triumph took place in the Roman Colosseum hence the inspiration for our Roman-style sammich. Turkey and Provolone on Focaccia, Tomatoes, Romaine and our secret blend of basil infused extra virgin olive oils.

"Dude. I love the Return of the Dragon Sammich, it is awesome!" - Debbie Dornhoefer

Desperado Sammich
Desperado—A bold or desperate outlaw. El Mariachi: “It's easier to pull the trigger than play guitar. Easier to destroy than create.” Hot Diced Chicken with Cheddar and Provolone on a Hoagie with our secret “South of the Border” sauce topped with fresh sliced jalapeños.

The Turkulese Sammich
A new Hero. Worshipped for it’s unique taste. The cult of Turkulese spread through the Roman world. It has now come to Austin, Texas. Turkulese is here now, just for you and only at Kick Butt Coffee Music & Booze Turkey and Cheddar with our Secret Citrus Aioli Sauce on Focaccia.

In the United States, the expression "say uncle" may be used to indicate submission, such as when wrestling, or a cry for mercy in a game. You'll "say uncle" when you 'give in' to this sammich. Hot Roast Beef and Cheddar on Ciabatta with Horse Radish Sauce.
